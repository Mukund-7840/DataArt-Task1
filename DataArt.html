Christ University Timeline

1969

Establishment of Christ College

Founded by Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI) in Bangalore, affiliated to Bangalore University.

1998

Autonomous Status Granted

UGC granted autonomous status, allowing Christ College to design its own curriculum.

2004

NAAC Accreditation (A+)

Received A+ grade from NAAC, marking its excellence in education.

2008

Declared as Deemed-to-be University

Christ College became Christ University under Section 3 of the UGC Act.

2014

Expansion: Kengeri Campus

New campus developed for Engineering, Management, and Sciences.

2019

Golden Jubilee (50 Years)

Celebrated 50 years of academic excellence and service to the nation.

2022

New Campuses: Yeshwanthpur & Bannerghatta

Launched two additional campuses to expand reach and offer more programs.

2023

National Ranking Milestone

Ranked among the top private universities in India by NIRF.

2025

Digital Transformation Initiatives

University embraces hybrid learning, AI integration, and sustainability efforts.
